Apple

Apples are lower in calories, higher in fiber and antioxidants, and can contribute to satiety and digestive health.

Vitamins

The main vitamin composition of apples per 100g is as follows:

  • Vitamin C: 4.6mg
  • Folate: 2.2mcg
  • Vitamin A: 54IU

Banana

Bananas are higher in potassium, carbohydrates, and calories, making them a good source of energy for pre-workout snacks or quick energy boosts.

Vitamins

The main vitamin composition of bananas per 100g is as follows:

  • Vitamin C: 8.7mg
  • Folate: 20mcg
  • Vitamin A: 64IU
